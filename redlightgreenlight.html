Instructions

How to play?

Click on the playarea to move towards the goal. You will move a small step per click only.

Beware, do not move when the girl sees you (or when the line is red) or you will be killed!

You will have 1 minute to reach the goal. Move fast before any other passes the goal!


How to win?

Be the first one to cross the line without being caught by the girl before the time runs out!
